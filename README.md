## Objective
1. Analyze sales performance across channels and regions.
2. Identify gaps between revenue and profitability.
3. Recommend strategies to improve business efficiency and growth.
   
## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel 📊
  - Data Cleaning
  - Pivot Tables
  - Data Visualization 

## Dataset
Source: Adidas Sales Dataset
- Data includes:
 - Channel (Sales Method)
 - Region
 - Units Sold
 - Total Sales
 - Operating Profit

## Data Preparation
- Removed irrelevant columns
- Standardized column names
- Ensured consistent data formats
- Created a cleaned dataset for analysis
 
## Insights
- The online channel drives the highest sales volume but delivers comparatively lower profit margins, indicating margin pressure in high-volume channels.
- Retail channels generate lower overall sales but demonstrate stronger profitability, suggesting higher customer purchase intent and better margin control.
- The divergence between revenue and profitability across channels highlights inefficiencies in channel strategy, where high sales volumes are not translating into proportional returns.
- Regional performance varies significantly, with certain regions underperforming and presenting potential opportunities for targeted growth strategies.

## Recommendation
- Optimize pricing and discount strategies in high-volume channels (e.g., online) to improve profit margins without significantly impacting sales.
- Increase focus on high-performing and high-margin channels to improve overall business efficiency and profitability.
- Identify underperforming regions and implement targeted marketing or promotional strategies to improve sales performance.
- Monitor the balance between revenue and profitability across channels regularly to support better data-driven decision-making.

## Conclusion 
While Adidas demonstrates strong revenue generation, the analysis reveals inconsistencies and gaps in profitability across channels and regions. A more balanced strategy focusing on margin optimization and targeted regional expansion can drive sustainable and efficient growth.
